About the Public Science Trust

To fund transformative NSF-aligned research and education projects that advance scientific knowledge, innovation, and societal well-being, empowering donors to support specific areas of impact. Our flagship initiative, 200 Earths, aims to develop sustainable mesocosm models to study ecosystems down to the molecular level while expanding students’ STEM skills.

Our Impact

Your donations support NSF-aligned projects that have transformed science and society. Here are some of the NSF’s major contributions your support helps continue:

Internet Development - Funded TCP/IP and NSFNET, foundational to the modern internet.
Synthetic Biology (SynBio) - Advanced bioengineering for sustainable agriculture and environmental remediation.
Long-Term Ecological Research (LTER) - Supports 28 sites studying ecosystems, informing climate and biodiversity policies.
LIGO Gravitational Waves - Detected ripples in spacetime, confirming Einstein’s theory.
Graduate Research Fellowship Program (GRFP) - Trained millions, fostering diverse STEM talent.
