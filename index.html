About the Public Science Trust

Our Mission

To fund transformative NSF-aligned research and education projects that advance scientific knowledge, innovation, and societal well-being, empowering donors to support specific areas of impact.

Bylaws

The Public Science Trust operates under a set of bylaws ensuring transparency, accountability, and alignment with our nonprofit mission. Key provisions include:

  • Governance: A board of directors oversees operations, with annual elections.
  • Fund Allocation: Donations are directed to NSF-aligned projects, with at least 80% of funds supporting research and education.
  • Transparency: Annual reports detail fund usage and project outcomes.
