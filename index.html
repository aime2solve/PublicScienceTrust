About the Public Science Trust

The Public Science Trust is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit committed to advancing scientific discovery and STEM education through public-supported, tax-deductible donations. Aligned with the National Science Foundation’s mission, we empower everyone to fund transformative research and education in 10 key areas. Our flagship initiative, 200 Earths, uses sustainable ecotrons to study ecosystems and engage students in hands-on learning.
